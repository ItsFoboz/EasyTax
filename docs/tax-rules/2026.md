# Bulgarian freelancer tax — 2026

First EUR tax year. Bulgaria adopted the euro on **2026-01-01** at the
official fixed parity **1 EUR = 1.95583 BGN**. Because the 2026 Budget
Act was not adopted in time, all main thresholds, contribution bases,
taxes, and benefits were "frozen" at their 2025 BGN levels and
automatically converted to euros at parity (rounded to two decimals
under the official methodology).

## Income tax (ЗДДФЛ)

- Flat rate: **10%**. Unchanged.
- 25% normative expense deduction: unchanged.

## Social security (КСО)

| Figure | 2025 BGN | 2026 EUR |
|---|---:|---:|
| Minimum monthly insurable base (self-insured) | 1 077 | **550.66** |
| Maximum monthly insurable base | 4 130 | **2 111.64** |
| Statutory minimum wage (employees) | 1 213 | **620.20** |

Rates unchanged: pension 14.8% (post-1959) / 19.8% (pre-1960), UPF 5%
(post-1959 only), health 8%, sickness/maternity 3.5%.

> Note: in 2026 the minimum wage (620.20 €) is *higher* than the minimum
> insurable income (550.66 €). For certain occupational groups whose
> tabled insurable income is below the minimum wage, social security is
> calculated on the minimum wage. v1 of EasyTax does not model
> occupational groups; freelancers and self-insured persons pay on
> `min_monthly_base`. Affects employees only, not freelancers.

## VAT (ЗДДС)

- Registration threshold: **51,130 €** (= 100,000 BGN at parity, officially published).
- Turnover monitoring switches to **calendar-year basis** (was rolling 12-month before 2026).
- Application within **7 days** of exceeding the threshold; review period reduced to 7 days.
- Registration date is now the day **after** crossing the threshold.

### New small-enterprise scheme (deferred — Phase 4+)

A VAT exemption regime for small enterprises is available at:

- Bulgarian threshold: ≤ 51,130 € annual turnover, OR
- EU threshold: ≤ 100,000 € across the EU (current and previous year).

Persons opt in via a registration with the NRA, receiving a "BG…-EX"
identifier. EasyTax v1 does not yet model this scheme — manual VAT-status
flag in onboarding still applies.

## Filing rules during euro transition

- Returns for **periods before 01.01.2026**: filed in BGN.
- Returns for **2026 and onward**: filed in EUR.
- **Corrective returns** use the currency valid for the period being corrected.
- 2025 financial statements: BGN. 2026+ statements: entirely in EUR.

## Deadlines

Same calendar as 2025. Annual declaration by 30 April 2027.
