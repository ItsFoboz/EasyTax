# Bulgarian freelancer tax — 2024

All figures in BGN unless noted. Authoritative source: Bulgarian Budget Act
2024, ЗДДФЛ, КСО, ЗДДС.

## Income tax (ЗДДФЛ)

- Flat rate: **10%** on taxable base.
- Liberal professions (свободни професии): **25% normative expense deduction**
  applied automatically — no receipts required (Art. 29(1)(3) ЗДДФЛ).
- Taxable base = gross − 25% normative − social security paid.

## Social security (КСО)

- Minimum monthly insurable base for self-insured: **933 BGN**.
- Maximum monthly insurable base: **3,750 BGN** (annual max **45,000 BGN**).
- Pension (ДОО, 1st pillar):
  - Born **before 1960**: 19.8%
  - Born **1960 or later**: 14.8%
- Universal Pension Fund (УПФ, 2nd pillar): **5%** — born 1960 or later only.
- Health (НЗОК): **8%**.
- Sickness/maternity (ОЗМ): **3.5%**.

Residual rule: if you already pay social security through an employer or your
own EOOD, you only owe contributions on the gap between that base and the
annual maximum, capped at your actual freelance income.

## VAT (ЗДДС)

- Registration threshold: **166,000 BGN** annual turnover.
- Above threshold → mandatory VAT registration within 7 days.
- App emits a warning at **80%** of threshold.

## Deadlines

| What | When |
|------|------|
| Quarterly advance Q1 | 15 April |
| Quarterly advance Q2 | 15 July |
| Quarterly advance Q3 | 15 October |
| Annual declaration (Art. 50) | 30 April of following year |
| Form 1 (monthly social security) | 25th of following month |
| Form 6 (annual social security) | 30 April of following year |

Q4 has no separate advance; the final reconciliation is settled in the
annual declaration.
